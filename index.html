Daniel Marrama

Hi. I am a bioinformatician currently working in the Peters Lab at the La Jolla Institute for Immunology.

I work on building tools for the IEDB and conducting analyses on immunological data.

With a strong personal connection to autoimmune diseases, I'm dedicated to working on solving these conditions. My dedication to this field is fueled by the desire to improve the lives of those affected by autoimmunity.
